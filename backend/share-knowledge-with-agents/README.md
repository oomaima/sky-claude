# 🧠 Share Knowledge with Agents

Welcome to the **GenVIZ Intelligence Hub**. This space is dedicated to collecting the collective wisdom of our team (Mainly Josep 😎) to make our AI agents smarter, more precise, and truly executive-grade.

### 🎯 Our Mission
We are building the future of airline intelligence. To do that, our agents need to learn from the best prompts, the most effective visualizations, and the "gotchas" discovered across different AI tools.

### 📥 What to Contribute
- **Prompt Snippets**: Examples of prompts that worked exceptionally well (or failed interestingly).
- **Screenshots**: Visual proof of great dashboard layouts or chart configurations.
- **Logic Insights**: Descriptions of how a specific complex DAX query was successfully generated.
- **Reference Material**: Documentation or patterns from other tools that we should emulate.

### 🚀 How to Share
Simply drop your files, screenshots, or `.txt` notes into this folder. The content will be used to fine-tune our system prompts and expand our few-shot examples.

**Your expertise is the fuel for our AI's evolution. Let's build something extraordinary together!** ✈️🔥
